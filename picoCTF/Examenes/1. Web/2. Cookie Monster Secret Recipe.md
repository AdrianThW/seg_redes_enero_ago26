#### Description

Cookie Monster has hidden his top-secret cookie recipe somewhere on his website. As an aspiring cookie detective, your mission is to uncover this delectable secret. Can you outsmart Cookie Monster and find the hidden recipe? You can access the Cookie Monster [here](http://verbal-sleep.picoctf.net:50743/) and good luck

Al encontrar la cookie ya sea con cookie editor o en el inspect del navegador encontramos el "value" que esta en base64, hay dos maneras de decodificarlo

### 1. Solucion
Usando burpsuite

1. Ir al apartado de Decoder
2. Pegar el texto y darle a decode as: "base64"

### 2. Usando la linea de comandos

```
adrian@ThW:~$ echo -n "cGljb0NURntjMDBrMWVfbTBuc3Rlcl9sMHZlc19jMDBraWVzXzczMTEwRUQxfQ" | base64 -d
picoCTF{c00k1e_m0nster_l0ves_}base64: invalid input
adrian@ThW:~$ 
```
