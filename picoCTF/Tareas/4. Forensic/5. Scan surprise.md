#### Description

I've gotten bored of handing out flags as text. Wouldn't it be cool if they were an image instead? You can download the challenge files here:

- [challenge.zip](https://artifacts.picoctf.net/c_atlas/14/challenge.zip)

Additional details will be available after launching your challenge instance.

Al descargar el zip está una imagen con un QR, se puede usar un celular que dará la flag o usar la herramienta zbar-tools, el comando zbarimg recibe el texto con la flag.
